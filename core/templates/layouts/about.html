Who We Are

Pakistan's Training Platform Built For Everyone

Traino — Training Aide is a web-based training management system designed to bridge the gap between trainers and trainees across Pakistan. We believe everyone deserves access to quality training from the comfort of their home.

Our platform supports health & fitness, technology, personal development and many other domains — making quality training accessible to all without geographical barriers.

Our Mission

To promote online training in Pakistan by providing a structured platform where individuals can access training services from their selected trainers — from home.

Our Vision

A Pakistan where every person — regardless of location, gender, or background — has equal access to professional training and skill development.
